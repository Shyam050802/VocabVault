VocabVault

Browser's Vocabulary Enricher

Here's how to use the extentsion

  1. Meaning Lookup:


    Access the definitions of complex words with a single click.


    Highlight the word and select the dictionary icon to get the meaning.


    Another way to get the meaning is to highlight the word, right-click, and select "Find meaning."


  2. Pronunciation Guide:


    Listen to the audio pronunciation of words and learn how to sound like a native speaker.


    To access other features follow the instructions above and select the "Read more" option to access the sidebar.


    You can find the Phonetic and a speaker icon for pronunciation.


  3. Parts of Speech:


    Get the grammatical classification and function of words for error-free text creation, with a definition and example for each classification.


    Access the sidebar to find various grammatical classifications for the selected word with a definition and example.
